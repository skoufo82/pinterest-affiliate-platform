## Getting Started

As a creator on our platform, you'll get:

- **Your Own Branded Storefront**: A unique URL (e.g., `yoursite.com/creator/your-name`) showcasing your curated products
- **Full Product Control**: Create, edit, and delete your own products
- **Theme Customization**: Personalize your storefront with custom colors and fonts
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Track page views, clicks, and product performance
- **Featured Products**: Highlight your best items at the top of your page

### What You'll Need

- A valid email address
- High-quality product images (JPEG, PNG, or WebP format)
- Amazon affiliate links for your products
- Social media profiles (optional but recommended)

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. Navigate to the platform homepage
2. Click the **"Become a Creator"** button in the header
3. Fill out the registration form:
   - **Username**: Choose a unique username (this will be part of your URL)
   - **Email**: Your email address for notifications
   - **Password**: Create a secure password (min 8 characters, include uppercase, lowercase, and numbers)
   - **Display Name**: The name that will appear on your storefront

![Creator Signup Form](docs/screenshots/creator-signup.png)

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

1. Check your email inbox for a verification message
2. Click the verification link
3. You'll be redirected to the login page

### Step 3: First Login

1. Log in with your email and password
2. You'll be taken to your creator dashboard

**Important**: Your username becomes your unique URL slug and **cannot be changed** after account creation. Choose carefully!

**URL Format**: `yoursite.com/creator/your-username`

**Username Requirements**:
- 3-20 characters
- Lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only
- No spaces or special characters
- Must be unique across all creators

## Setting Up Your Profile

Your profile is the first thing visitors see when they land on your storefront. Make it count!

### Step 1: Access Profile Settings

1. From your creator dashboard, click **"Profile Settings"** in the sidebar
2. You'll see the profile editor with several sections

### Step 2: Add Your Display Name and Bio

**Display Name** (Required)
- This is your brand name that appears at the top of your storefront
- Maximum 100 characters
- Example: "Sarah's Home Finds" or "Tech Gadgets by Mike"

**Bio** (Required)
- Tell visitors about yourself and what you curate
- Maximum 500 characters
- Be authentic and engaging
- Example: "Hi! I'm Sarah, and I love finding beautiful, functional home decor pieces that won't break the bank. Follow along for my latest finds!"

![Profile Editor](docs/screenshots/profile-editor.png)

### Step 3: Upload Images

**Profile Image** (Required)
- Square format recommended (500x500px minimum)
- Maximum file size: 5MB
- Formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP
- This appears as your avatar throughout the platform

**Cover Image** (Optional but recommended)
- Wide format (1200x400px recommended)
- Maximum file size: 5MB
- This is the hero image at the top of your storefront
- Choose something that represents your brand aesthetic

**How to Upload**:
1. Click the upload area or drag and drop your image
2. Preview the image before saving
3. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Step 4: Add Social Media Links

Connect your social media profiles to build trust and grow your audience:

- **Instagram**: Your Instagram profile URL
- **Pinterest**: Your Pinterest profile URL
- **TikTok**: Your TikTok profile URL

These will appear as clickable icons on your storefront.

**Format**: Must be full URLs (e.g., `https://instagram.com/yourusername`)

### Step 5: Save Your Profile

Click the **"Save Profile"** button at the bottom of the page. You'll see a success message when your changes are saved.

## Customizing Your Theme

Make your storefront uniquely yours with custom colors and fonts!

### Step 1: Access Theme Settings

In the Profile Settings page, scroll to the **"Theme Customization"** section.

### Step 2: Choose Your Colors

**Primary Color**
- This is your main brand color
- Used for headers, buttons, and accents
- Click the color picker to choose your color
- Enter a hex code directly (e.g., #2C5F2D)

**Accent Color**
- A complementary color for highlights and CTAs
- Should contrast well with your primary color
- Used for links and interactive elements

**Color Tips**:
- Use colors that reflect your brand personality
- Ensure good contrast for readability
- Test on both light and dark backgrounds
- Consider accessibility (avoid low-contrast combinations)

### Step 3: Select Your Font

Choose from our curated font selection:
- **Inter**: Modern, clean, great for tech/minimalist brands
- **Playfair Display**: Elegant, sophisticated, perfect for luxury/fashion
- **Roboto**: Versatile, readable, works for any niche
- **Montserrat**: Bold, contemporary, ideal for lifestyle brands

### Step 4: Preview Your Theme

As you make changes, you'll see a live preview of how your storefront will look. Make adjustments until you're happy with the result.

### Step 5: Save Your Theme

Click **"Save Changes"** to apply your theme to your public storefront.

## Adding Your First Products

Now for the fun part - adding products to your storefront!

### Step 1: Navigate to Product Manager

1. Click **"My Products"** in the sidebar
2. Click the **"Add New Product"** button

### Step 2: Fill Out Product Details

**Title** (Required)
- Clear, descriptive product name
- Maximum 200 characters
- Example: "Stainless Steel Insulated Water Bottle - 32oz"

**Description** (Required)
- Detailed product information
- Maximum 2000 characters
- Include key features, benefits, and why you love it
- Be honest and authentic

**Category** (Required)
- Select the most appropriate category
- Available categories: Home & Kitchen, Tech & Electronics, Fashion & Beauty, Health & Wellness, etc.
- Products are filterable by category on your storefront

**Price** (Optional)
- Enter the current price (e.g., 29.99)
- This is for display only - actual purchases happen on Amazon
- Prices can be automatically synced with Amazon (if enabled)

![Product Form](docs/screenshots/product-form.png)

### Step 3: Upload Product Image

**Image Requirements**:
- High quality, clear product photo
- Minimum 800x800px recommended
- Maximum file size: 5MB
- Formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP
- Show the product clearly
- Avoid watermarks or excessive text overlays

**How to Upload**:
1. Click the upload area or drag and drop
2. Preview the image
3. Adjust if needed

### Step 4: Add Amazon Affiliate Link

**Important**: This is how you earn commissions!

**How to Get Your Amazon Affiliate Link**:
1. Log into your Amazon Associates account
2. Search for the product on Amazon
3. Generate your affiliate link
4. Copy the full link (should include your affiliate tag)

**Link Format**: `https://amazon.com/dp/PRODUCTID?tag=your-tag-20`

**The platform will automatically extract the ASIN** from your link, so you can paste the full URL.

### Step 5: Mark as Featured (Optional)

Toggle the **"Featured Product"** switch if you want this product to appear in the featured section at the top of your storefront.

**Featured Product Tips**:
- Limit to 3-6 featured products for best impact
- Feature your best-selling or highest-quality items
- Update featured products regularly to keep content fresh

### Step 6: Submit for Review

Click **"Create Product"** to submit your product for admin review.

**What Happens Next**:
1. Your product is submitted with status "Pending"
2. Platform admins review your product (usually within 24-48 hours)
3. You'll receive an email when your product is approved or if changes are needed
4. Once approved, your product appears on your public storefront

## Understanding the Approval Process

All products go through a quality review before appearing on your public storefront. This ensures a high-quality experience for all visitors.

### Why We Review Products

- Maintain platform quality standards
- Ensure images meet quality requirements
- Verify affiliate links are properly formatted
- Check for appropriate content and descriptions

### Product Statuses

**Pending** üü°
- Product is awaiting admin review
- Not visible on your public storefront
- You can still edit the product

**Approved** üü¢
- Product passed review
- Visible on your public storefront
- You can continue to edit (changes may require re-approval)

**Rejected** üî¥
- Product did not meet quality standards
- Not visible on your public storefront
- You'll receive an email with the reason for rejection
- You can edit and resubmit

### Common Rejection Reasons

1. **Low-Quality Images**
   - Image is blurry, pixelated, or too small
   - **Solution**: Upload a higher resolution image (minimum 800x800px)

2. **Incomplete Description**
   - Description is too short or lacks detail
   - **Solution**: Add more information about features, benefits, and why you recommend it

3. **Invalid Amazon Link**
   - Link is broken or doesn't include affiliate tag
   - **Solution**: Double-check your link and ensure it includes your affiliate tag

4. **Inappropriate Content**
   - Product violates platform guidelines
   - **Solution**: Review our content policy and choose appropriate products

5. **Duplicate Product**
   - You've already added this product
   - **Solution**: Edit your existing product instead of creating a duplicate

### What to Do If Your Product Is Rejected

1. Check your email for the rejection reason
2. Go to **"My Products"** and find the rejected product
3. Click **"Edit"** to make changes
4. Address the issues mentioned in the rejection reason
5. Click **"Update Product"** to resubmit for review

**Tip**: If you're unsure about the rejection reason, you can contact support for clarification.

## Viewing Your Analytics

Track your storefront's performance with our analytics dashboard.

### Accessing Analytics

1. Click **"Analytics"** in the sidebar
2. Select your date range (default: last 30 days)

### Key Metrics

**Page Views**
- Total number of times your storefront was viewed
- Includes unique and repeat visitors
- Trend chart shows daily page views over time

**Product Views**
- Total number of times your products were viewed
- Includes views from your storefront and product detail pages

**Affiliate Clicks**
- Number of times visitors clicked your Amazon affiliate links
- This is the most important metric for earning potential

**Click-Through Rate (CTR)**
- Percentage of product views that resulted in clicks
- Formula: (Affiliate Clicks / Product Views) √ó 100
- Higher CTR indicates more engaging products

![Analytics Dashboard](docs/screenshots/analytics-dashboard.png)

### Top Products

See which products are performing best:

**Top by Views**
- Products with the most views
- Indicates which products attract the most attention

**Top by Clicks**
- Products with the most affiliate clicks
- Shows which products convert best

**Top by CTR**
- Products with the highest click-through rate
- Reveals your most compelling product presentations

### Using Analytics to Improve

**If page views are low**:
- Share your storefront link on social media
- Optimize your profile bio and images
- Engage with your audience on other platforms

**If product views are low**:
- Add more products to your catalog
- Improve product titles and descriptions
- Use better product images

**If affiliate clicks are low**:
- Feature your best products
- Write more compelling product descriptions
- Ensure prices are competitive
- Add more context about why you recommend each product

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in

**Possible solutions**:
1. Check that you're using the correct email address
2. Try resetting your password
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser
5. Check if your account has been disabled (you'll receive an email)

### My product image won't upload

**Possible causes**:
- File is too large (max 5MB)
- File format is not supported (use JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
- Slow internet connection

**Solutions**:
1. Compress your image using a tool like TinyPNG
2. Convert to a supported format
3. Try uploading on a faster connection

### My product was rejected

**What to do**:
1. Check your email for the specific rejection reason
2. Edit your product to address the issues
3. Resubmit for review
4. If unclear, contact support for guidance

### My analytics aren't updating

**Possible causes**:
- Analytics are aggregated daily (updates may take 24 hours)
- No recent traffic to your storefront
- Date range is set incorrectly

**Solutions**:
1. Wait 24 hours for data to aggregate
2. Check your date range settings
3. Verify you're looking at the correct metrics

### My theme changes aren't showing

**Solutions**:
1. Make sure you clicked "Save Changes"
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try viewing in an incognito/private window
4. Wait a few minutes for changes to propagate

### I want to change my username/slug

Unfortunately, usernames cannot be changed after account creation as they're part of your public URL. This ensures consistency for your audience.

**Alternative**: If you absolutely need a different username, you can:
1. Create a new creator account with the desired username
2. Contact support to migrate your products (if possible)
3. Note: Analytics history cannot be transferred

### I'm not receiving email notifications

**Check**:
1. Your spam/junk folder
2. That your email address is correct in your profile
3. Your notification preferences (coming soon)

**Solution**: Add our email domain to your safe senders list
